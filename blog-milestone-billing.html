Why Milestone-Based Billing Keeps Your Project on Track

By Sahyadri PMO Team • Residential & Commercial Projects

Cash flow is one of the biggest worries for clients. At Sahyadri, we use milestone-based billing so that payments are always linked to visible, measurable progress on site.

How we break up a typical project

  • Advance for design and mobilization
  • Plinth completion, each slab, and masonry milestones
  • Plastering, flooring, and finishing packages
  • Final handover after snag list closure

Benefits for you as a client

You only pay after a milestone is completed and jointly certified. This keeps the contractor accountable, gives you clear visibility, and makes bank loan disbursements easier.

Our documentation

Every milestone is supported by BOQs, site photographs, and quality checklists. This becomes a permanent record for you and your banker.
