Choosing the Right Block for Cooler, Quieter Homes

By Sahyadri Design Team • Mandya, Mysuru & Bengaluru

Wall material plays a huge role in how hot, noisy, and efficient your home feels. In our region, we commonly evaluate AAC blocks, fly ash bricks, and traditional clay blocks based on thermal comfort, sound insulation, and cost.

AAC vs Fly Ash vs Clay

  • AAC blocks – Light weight, good thermal insulation, ideal for upper floors.
  • Fly ash bricks – Denser, good for load-bearing walls and budget-conscious projects.
  • Clay blocks – Traditional look, good breathability, slightly higher cost.

What we recommend for Mandya & Mysuru climates

For most G+1 and G+2 homes, we prefer a mix – AAC on external walls for heat reduction and fly-ash or solid blocks for internal partitions where sound privacy is critical.

How Sahyadri decides for your project

We look at orientation, elevation design, local availability, and your budget before locking a walling system. This is always explained in your BOQ so you know what you’re paying for.
